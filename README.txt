Last Release: 15 June 2016
Latest version: 1.0
Developer: Justin Kent

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                       About
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Fast Fourier Transform multiplies two large polynomials together to create a combined
product polynomail. The FFT() creates solutions for each polynomial using a divide and
conquer method splitting the arrays into odd & even sections. A solution is calculated
based on multiplying the roots of unity.
